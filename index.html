Brief Work History:

Hello, my name is John Chiodo, I started my career in telecommunications with AT&T in 1987, the company went thru many changes and so did my skill set. I originally came from a hardware backround and a few of my early positions at AT&T were hardware design related.

Thoughout the years I shifted my skills to programming in assembly and C. I found an opening in the wireless division and soon realized there was a void in the testing area. I applied my skills to unit level and cluster/network level testing.

I have worked with many different cell types, servers, cellular protocols, mobile phones and networks.
